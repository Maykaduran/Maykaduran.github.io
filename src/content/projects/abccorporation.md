## Project Overview

This project analyzes employee data from the fictional company **ABC Corporation**, a tech consultancy specialized in AI and machine learning.

The objective was to identify the key factors influencing **employee satisfaction and attrition**, providing insights that could help improve talent retention and workplace wellbeing.

The analysis combines **data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), and data visualization** to transform raw HR data into actionable insights for decision-making.

## Objectives

- Audit and clean HR datasets to ensure data quality.
- Identify factors that influence employee turnover.
- Visualize trends in employee satisfaction, compensation, and roles.
- Provide recommendations to improve employee retention.

---

## Dataset

The dataset contains anonymized employee information including:

- Demographic data (age, gender)
- Department and job role
- Monthly income and compensation
- Work-life balance and job satisfaction
- Employee tenure
- Attrition status (whether employees left the company)

--- 

## Key Insights

### Department Attrition Analysis

Sales and Human Resources departments show the **highest employee turnover rates**, suggesting potential structural or workload issues.

### Salary and Attrition Relationship

Employees who left the company tend to have **lower monthly income**, particularly in Sales and HR departments.

### Job Role Risk Factors

Certain roles show significantly higher attrition rates:

- Sales Representatives
- Laboratory Technicians

These roles may require targeted retention strategies.
